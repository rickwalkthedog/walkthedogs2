Meet Your Dog Walker

Hi, I'm Rick. After retiring, I decided to combine my love of dogs with a service that helps pet owners feel confident their pets are receiving the attention and exercise they deserve.

As a local Belton resident, I understand how important it is to find someone trustworthy to care for your pets. Every dog receives the same care and attention I would give my own.

My goal is simple: happy dogs and happy owners.
